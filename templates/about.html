About me

Who am I?

Hi! I am David, a self-taught programmer. I have been doing this for multiple years simply because I am fascinated by the art of coding. I finished the American University of Armenia, Communications department. Being in university has taught me many things, but most importantly I learned that one can pick up any profession as long as there is passion. I have tried many things including photography, writing, playing piano, PR, journalism, however nothing ever possessed me as much as programming

What do I do?

I use Python, Ruby, Javascript and Java to build most of my works. I love automating everything, writing scripts to perform the simplest and the most difficult tasks. I love challenges and often start projects without having any idea on how to do them, however I enjoy learning and this makes me engaged in my projects. I build scripts, web applications, create software, build simple games and automate tasks.

Any hobbies?

I have a lot of hobbies. While I didn't pursue my career in photography, I still enjoy it as a hobby. Also I love reading books (Albert Camus fan), playing video games (Starcraft), learning anything related to IT and hiking.
